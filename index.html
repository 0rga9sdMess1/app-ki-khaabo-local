🍲 Why Ki Khaabo Exists

Some nights you want comfort.

Some nights you want health.

Some nights you just want Maggi and silence.

Ki Khaabo? was born from real dinner table confusion: “What should we eat tonight?” “Will this survive tomorrow lunch?” “Didn’t we just have paneer yesterday?”

Between work, tiredness, moods, leftovers and cravings, food becomes less about recipes and more about emotional logistics.

This app tries to help with that gently.
